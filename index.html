About SOC Toolkit

      

        SOC Toolkit is a free, browser-based utility designed to assist security
        analysts, incident responders, and threat researchers in daily investigations.
      

      

        The project is maintained by an independent security professional and follows
        a community-driven approach. Feedback and improvements are welcome.
      

    


    

    
Privacy Policy

    

        We do not collect, store, or process any information entered by users into this website.
        This includes, but is not limited to URLs, IP addresses, domains, hashes, text, or any other indicators submitted through the tool.
        All user-provided data is processed locally in the user's browser and is never transmitted to, logged by, or stored on our servers.
    

    

        We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics such as page views, browser type, and general geographic region.
        No user-submitted data is shared with Google Analytics.
    

    

        When users choose to open external services or third-party tools, their interaction is subject to the privacy policies and data handling practices of those respective services.
